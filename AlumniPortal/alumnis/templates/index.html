Alumni-Connect is an association of graduates or, more broadly, of former students (alumni).These associations often organize social events, publish newsletters or magazines, and raise funds for the organization.Many provide a variety of benefits and services that help alumni maintain connections to their educational institution and fellow graduates.

TKM College Of Engineering is one of the most distinctive colleges in Kerala. For Alumni, the years spent as a student are among the most exciting and challenging of our lives. We all leave TKM with strong feelings about the College and a sense that we have formed a unique identity.One of the purposes of the Alumni Connect is to encourage and promote that connection. Its goal is to keep that connection alive and to give it a practical quality by organizing programs and services for Alumni. Another purpose is to represent Alumni views to the College and to develop ways that Alumni can be involved with current students.

The founder Principal of the TKM Dr.T A Shahul Hameed took special care to enhance the reputation of TKM Alumni-Connect. With the strong support by the TKM Management and the continuous involvement of the Principal and the faculty of TKM, the alumni association is carrying out various tasks throughout the year. These tasks aim at not only bringing the members to a close family atmosphere but also carrying out various service activities.Every year the TKM Alumni Connect organizes events like Alumni Day, Young Alumni Meet, Technical Meetings, Entrepreneurship development programmes and product exhibitions.
